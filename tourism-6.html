Expeditions
TO THE NORTH POLE

North Pole dogsled

North Pole dogsled

Duration: 10-12 days

Prices per person: 22.000 EUR

1st day
Meeting with a guide, check your individual and group gear, general instruction. Preparation for the training trip and packing the sleds. Overnight at the hotel.
2nt day
Training trip in the Longyearbyen surrounding. Overnight at the hotel.
Days 3-9
Ski trek to the North Pole, overnight sleeping in the expedition tents on the drifting ice. The start point will be decided in dependence on the ice and prevailing ice drift conditions. This tour contemplates not only dogsled driving but also a lot of skiing.
Day 10
On arrival at the top of the globe, we will celebrate it. Pick up by charter helicopter and fly back to Barneo Ice Camp. Coffee, tea, and snacks for free and strong drinks for sale will be available in Barneo mess room while waiting for the plane from Longyearbyen. Usually, depending on the weather, it takes a couple hours. Flight from Barneo to Longyearbyen on the AN-74 aircraft (approx. flight duration 2 h 30 min).Transfer to your hotel. Night at the hotel.
Day 11
Free time. You have a chance to take part in Svalbard adventure activities (not included, advance booking required). Independent transfer to the airport for your flight home.

Please note: all charter flights are heavily weather dependent!

  • This style of polar exploration can be a very difficult and it requires excellent cardiovascular endurance. Instead of riding on the back of the sled you more frequently work alongside the dogs, guiding them over pressure ridges and around open water leads. You'll be participating in all aspects of the expedition, including setting up camp, cooking, feeding the dogs, etc.

LIST OF EQUIPMENT
for the expedition to the North Pole

You should bring your own:

  • • Winter coat and trousers with Thinsulate insulation: 1 set
  • • Thin underwear made of Polycolon (so-called thermal underwear): 1 set
  • • Fleece jacket and trousers made of Polartec 300 or Polartec 200
  • • Thick expedition socks: 2 pairs
  • • Sunglasses 100% UV & IR: 1
  • • Sun protection cream: SPF 40
  • • Hygienic lipstick: SPF 40
  • • Personal medical kit
  • • Expedition footwear: 1 pair
  • • Expedition mittens: 1 pair
  • • Woolen hat with Windstopper membrane: 1

ATTENTION:

We advise you optional basic polar expedition clothes kit, but everyone endures cold differently. Therefore, if you have some items in your wardrobe, which are tested in cold environment and suit you, you are welcome to bring them.
